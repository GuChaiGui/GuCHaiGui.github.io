_ coins: 1, 5, 10, 50, 100 and 500

Transport

Plane Ticket

- Price for a direct flight France - Japan
Allow 11 to 12 hours of direct flight time, with one or two meals on board. The average price of a direct flight is 1000 EUR / pers. in regular season.

- Price of a flight with stopover France - Japan
Count from 14 to 18 hours of flight with transit and waiting time in the main airport of the airline company. The average price of a flight with stopover is around 800 EUR / pers. in regular season.

- Price of a discount flight France - Japan
Expect flight schedules in the evening or very early in the morning, as well as significant transit waiting time, in the order of 4 to 8 hours. On the other hand, the price of your plane ticket to Japan will be really attractive, around 600 EUR / pers. in regular season.

Train

It is the most convenient (and economical) way to travel within Japan.

The country has one of the most developed rail networks in the world. Trains are on time, and the numerous frequencies make it possible to move quickly and easily from one city to another.
When arriving at the stations (large or small), buses, funiculars (in the mountains) or racks are waiting for passengers. You can get from one to the other without difficulty.
Tickets can be bought from ticket machines or from the green striped ticket machines located next to them. When in doubt, ask a friendly staff member, who often provides information in English and has an amazing sense of service.

Japan Rail Pass

The Japan Rail Pass, a package sold by the company JR, is interesting (and even indispensable) for travelers wishing to move around the country.

- Fares: economy class (regular), 7 days ¥29,110 (€243); 14 days ¥46,390 (€387); 21 days ¥59,350 (€495); discount 6-11 years.

The subway

In the cities imporétro is ultramodern and works very well.

However, avoid rush hours, as the cars are crowded with people, where they pile up "japanese style". Most major cities in Japan have their own subway system.
Tickets can be purchased at ticket machines, and prices vary depending on the distance and number of stations.

- Rates: in Tokyo, a ticket is worth at least ¥170 (€1.40) from 1 to 6 km, ¥200 (€1.70) from 7 to 11 km . Fares vary according to the companies. We strongly advise you to buy Suica or Pasmo cards in Tokyo. It is now valid in most urban transports in other cities in Japan.

- Subway maps : subway maps of the main cities of Japan can be found on the website of the National Tourist Office of Japan.

The bus

A very convenient means of transportation in some cities. In Tokyo, however, it is better to take the subway and train.

The bus system works very well. Efficiency, organization, modernity and sense of service.
City bus companies publish clear and practical plans. Tickets can be purchased on the bus.

Each trip will cost you 230 yens (about 1€80) for an adult, and 120 yens (0,95 €) for a child.
