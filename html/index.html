History Section

Your name

Your name or Kimi no nawa(君の名は。) in japanese, it's a Japanese animated film released on August 26, 2016 and directed by Makoto Shinkai which was adapted from his novel published on June 18, 2016 in Japan. The film was released on December 28, 2016 in France at the cinema (if you are a real fan you saw the movie at the cinema).

This film makes a lot of reference to The Garden of Words , one of his previous works for example Yukari Yukino, one of the main characters of The Garden of Words is Mitsuha's teacher.

The film has received critical acclaim, being praised for its animation and emotional impact, and was also a commercial success, becoming the fourth highest-grossing film of all time in Japan and the highest-grossing anime film worldwide, with, as of January 15, 2017, a gross of over $330 million USD.

Synopsis

A comet appears and mysteriously affects and connects the lives of two teenagers of the same age, a boy in the big, bustling city of Tokyo and a girl in a country village where life is slow but idyllic. They find for unknown reasons, they wake up in each other's bodies for weeks at a time. At first, they both think these experiences are just vivid dreams, but when the reality of their situations sinks in, they learn to adjust and even enjoy it. Soon they start to communicate and try to leave notes about who they are and what they are doing. But as they discover more about each other and the other's life, they uncover some disturbing hints that their distance is more than just physical and tragedy haunts them.

Characters

Mitsuha Miyamizu

Mitsuha Miyamizu is a high school girl dissatisfied with her life in Itomori, a mountainous and rural town of Gifu Prefecture. Mitsuha wishes to have a better life in Tokyo. She has a role as a shrine maiden (miko) in rituals for her mother's family shrine.

Taki Tachibana

Taki Tachibana is a high school boy in Tokyo. He works in a part-time job as a waiter at the Italian restaurant "Il Giardino delle Parole". He is a talented sketch artist and has aspirations to be an architect.
